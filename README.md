# Founder Fundraising OS

**Everything a founder needs to raise a round — from deciding the stage to wired funds.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

The complete operating system for raising venture capital, end to end. Decide your stage, amount, and valuation; run an honest readiness audit; model your cap table and dilution; build the pitch deck and narrative; size the market and financials; assemble a staged data room; shape the team story and key hires. Then run the raise like a sales process: target the right VCs and angels with warm paths, manage the pipeline to manufacture momentum, drill the pitch and the hard Q&A, pass diligence, negotiate the term sheet, choose SAFE vs priced, and drive the close to cash. Every skill is operator-grade with concrete checklists and deliverables.

## Install

- **From the catalog:** [skillme.dev/pack/founder-fundraising-os](https://skillme.dev/pack/founder-fundraising-os) — install the whole pack into Claude in one step.

## Skills in this pack

- **[Fundraising Stage Selector](skills/fundraising-stage-selector/SKILL.md)** — Decide your round, how much to raise, and a defensible valuation before you build a single slide.
- **[Fundraise Readiness Audit](skills/fundraise-readiness-audit/SKILL.md)** — Run an honest go/no-go audit across metrics, story, team, and legal hygiene before you start the raise.
- **[Cap Table Manager](skills/cap-table-manager/SKILL.md)** — Model your cap table and dilution across SAFEs, option pools, and priced rounds so you know what you actually own.
- **[Market Sizing](skills/market-sizing/SKILL.md)** — Builds defensible TAM/SAM/SOM models with bottom-up and top-down triangulation.
- **[Personal Financial Planner](skills/financial-planner/SKILL.md)** — Budgeting, debt payoff, and savings planning grounded in the user's real numbers.
- **[Fundraising Narrative](skills/fundraising-narrative/SKILL.md)** — Crafts the founder story arc for seed and Series A — origin, insight, traction, vision.
- **[Pitch Deck Builder](skills/pitch-deck-builder/SKILL.md)** — Build the investor pitch deck slide by slide — the 11 slides VCs expect, in the order they expect them.
- **[Data Room Builder](skills/data-room-builder/SKILL.md)** — Assemble and stage the diligence data room so confirmatory diligence speeds the close instead of stalling it.
- **[Fundraise Team & Hiring](skills/fundraise-team-hiring/SKILL.md)** — Build the team story investors fund and make the key hires that de-risk the round — with the right equity.
- **[Investor Targeting](skills/investor-targeting/SKILL.md)** — Build a ranked target list of the right VCs and angels and map the warm path to each before you send a single email.
- **[VC Pitch Email](skills/vc-pitch-email/SKILL.md)** — Writes cold VC outreach emails: traction first, ask last, under 100 words.
- **[Investor Pipeline CRM](skills/investor-pipeline-crm/SKILL.md)** — Run the raise as a tight sales pipeline — stages, cadence, and timing that manufacture momentum and competitive tension.
- **[Investor Update Writer](skills/investor-update-writer/SKILL.md)** — Monthly and quarterly investor updates: traction metrics, team highlights, ask, and forward-looking view.
- **[Pitch Practice Coach](skills/pitch-practice-coach/SKILL.md)** — Rehearse the pitch and drill the hard investor Q&A until the founder is calm, tight, and unflappable in the room.
- **[Series A Readiness](skills/series-a-readiness/SKILL.md)** — Assesses Series A readiness: metrics, narrative, team, and data room preparation.
- **[Due Diligence Checklist](skills/due-diligence-checklist/SKILL.md)** — Runs through commercial, financial, technical, and legal due diligence for investments.
- **[Term Sheet Explainer](skills/term-sheet-explainer/SKILL.md)** — Explains VC term sheet clauses in plain English with negotiation guidance.
- **[SAFE vs Priced Round](skills/safe-vs-priced-round/SKILL.md)** — Choose the right financing instrument and understand the mechanics — caps, discounts, MFN, pre vs post-money SAFEs, and priced equity.
- **[Term Sheet Negotiation](skills/term-sheet-negotiation/SKILL.md)** — Negotiate valuation and the terms that actually matter — preferences, board, protective provisions, pool — with real leverage.
- **[Fundraise Closing Mechanics](skills/fundraise-closing-mechanics/SKILL.md)** — Drive the deal from signed term sheet through confirmatory diligence, legal docs, and wired funds without losing momentum.
