# Founder Fundraising OS

**Everything a founder needs to raise a round — from deciding the stage to wired funds.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

The complete operating system for raising venture capital, end to end. Decide your stage, amount, and valuation; run an honest readiness audit; model your cap table and dilution; build the pitch deck and narrative; size the market and financials; assemble a staged data room; shape the team story and key hires. Then run the raise like a sales process: target the right VCs and angels with warm paths, manage the pipeline to manufacture momentum, drill the pitch and the hard Q&A, pass diligence, negotiate the term sheet, choose SAFE vs priced, and drive the close to cash. Every skill is operator-grade with concrete checklists and deliverables.

## Skills in this pack

- **[Fundraising Stage Selector](skills/fundraising-stage-selector/SKILL.md)** — Decide your round, how much to raise, and a defensible valuation before you build a single slide.
- **[Fundraise Readiness Audit](skills/fundraise-readiness-audit/SKILL.md)** — Runs the audit an investor will run before a founder starts pitching — scoring metrics, story, team, and legal/financial hygiene red/yellow/green and returning a go, fix-first, or wait verdict with the milestones that turn each red gate green.
- **[Cap Table Manager](skills/cap-table-manager/SKILL.md)** — Model your cap table and dilution across SAFEs, option pools, and priced rounds so you know what you actually own.
- **[Market Sizing](skills/market-sizing/SKILL.md)** — Builds a defensible TAM/SAM/SOM market-sizing model with bottom-up and top-down triangulation and a low/base/high sensitivity range.
- **[Personal Financial Planner](skills/financial-planner/SKILL.md)** — Builds a complete personal financial plan — a prioritized order of operations across budgeting, emergency savings, debt payoff, and goals — from the user's actual numbers, then routes each piece to the right specialist skill.
- **[Fundraising Narrative](skills/fundraising-narrative/SKILL.md)** — Builds the five-beat narrative spine — origin, insight, wedge, traction, vision — that answers why now, why you, and why this before any deck exists.
- **[Pitch Deck Builder](skills/pitch-deck-builder/SKILL.md)** — Build the investor pitch deck slide by slide — the 11 slides VCs expect, in the order they expect them.
- **[Data Room Builder](skills/data-room-builder/SKILL.md)** — Use when a founder needs to prepare materials for investor due diligence.
- **[Fundraise Team & Hiring](skills/fundraise-team-hiring/SKILL.md)** — Build the team story investors fund and make the key hires that de-risk the round — with the right equity.
- **[Investor Targeting](skills/investor-targeting/SKILL.md)** — Use when a founder needs to decide which investors to approach and how to reach them.
- **[VC Pitch Email](skills/vc-pitch-email/SKILL.md)** — Writes cold investor outreach under 100 words — a subject line built on the company's strongest verifiable metric, a personalized why-them opening line, traction before the ask, and a low-friction meeting request — delivered as three subject options, the email body with word count, and a follow-up line for five days later.
- **[Investor Pipeline CRM](skills/investor-pipeline-crm/SKILL.md)** — Run the raise as a tight sales pipeline — stages, cadence, and timing that manufacture momentum and competitive tension.
- **[Investor Update Writer](skills/investor-update-writer/SKILL.md)** — Drafts a monthly or quarterly investor update — TL;DR, consistent metric block (revenue, growth rate, burn, runway), highlights, honest lowlights, a specific ask, and next-period priorities — in a repeatable one-screen format.
- **[Pitch Practice Coach](skills/pitch-practice-coach/SKILL.md)** — Rehearse the pitch and drill the hard investor Q&A until the founder is calm, tight, and unflappable in the room.
- **[Series A Readiness](skills/series-a-readiness/SKILL.md)** — Audits whether a seed-stage company clears the Series A bar — benchmark metrics, narrative, team, and data room — and produces a red/yellow/green readiness scorecard with a 90-day gap-closing plan.
- **[Due Diligence Checklist](skills/due-diligence-checklist/SKILL.md)** — Runs structured diligence on an investment or acquisition target across four workstreams — commercial, financial, technical, and legal — and produces a red-flag register split into deal-killers versus manageable risks with a go/no-go recommendation.
- **[Term Sheet Explainer](skills/term-sheet-explainer/SKILL.md)** — Decodes every clause of a VC term sheet into plain English, marks each term founder-favorable, market-standard, or investor-favorable, and flags deviations in priority order.
- **[SAFE vs Priced Round](skills/safe-vs-priced-round/SKILL.md)** — Use when a founder is choosing how to structure a raise.
- **[Term Sheet Negotiation](skills/term-sheet-negotiation/SKILL.md)** — Negotiate valuation and the terms that actually matter — preferences, board, protective provisions, pool — with real leverage.
- **[Fundraise Closing Mechanics](skills/fundraise-closing-mechanics/SKILL.md)** — Drives a fundraise from signed term sheet to wired money — a closing checklist covering confirmatory diligence with same-day response discipline, the definitive-docs list (stock purchase agreement, charter, investor rights, board consents), closing conditions with owners and dates, re-trade defense, multi-party sequencing behind the lead, and post-wire actions like share issuance and the first investor update.
